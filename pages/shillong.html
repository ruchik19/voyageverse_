SHILLONG

Shillong, the capital of Meghalaya, is a charming hill station often referred to as the "Scotland of the East" for its rolling hills, lush greenery, and serene lakes. Nestled at an altitude of 1,525 meters, Shillong boasts a pleasant climate, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. It is a perfect destination for nature enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and those seeking a relaxing getaway.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

PLACES TO GO

    1. Elephanata Falls

  • A picturesque three-tiered waterfall located about 12 km from the city, offering scenic beauty and photo opportunities.

    • 2. Umiam Lake

  • Also known as Barapani, this man-made lake is ideal for boating, picnicking, and enjoying serene natural surroundings.

    • 3.Shillong Peak

  • The highest point in Shillong, providing panoramic views of the city and the surrounding countryside.

    4. Don Bosco Museum

  • A cultural museum showcasing the rich heritage of Northeast India through various exhibits and galleries.

    • 5. Ward's Lake

  • An artificial lake surrounded by a well-maintained garden, perfect for leisurely walks and boating.

    6. Living Root Bridges

  • Unique to Meghalaya, these bridges are made from the roots of rubber trees and are a testament to indigenous engineering.

    • 7. Cherrapunji

  • Known for its heavy rainfall, it boasts attractions like Nohkalikai Falls, Mawsmai Cave, and lush landscapes.
