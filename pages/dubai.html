DUBAI

Everything feels extra spectacular in Dubai—from the ultra-modern Burj Khalifa to the souks and malls filled with gold and jewellery vendors. It’s a place where if you can dream it, you can do it: Whether that means skiing indoors, dune-surfing in the desert, or zip-lining above the city. But it’s not all glitz and adrenaline-pumping action. Stroll through the winding alleys of Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood to see what Dubai was like during the mid-19th century. Or visit the Jumeirah Mosque (one of the few mosques open to non-Muslims) and learn about Emirati culture. Spot some street art on Jumeirah Beach Road and grab a bite at a shawarma shop, or spend the day hunting for spices and perfume then round things out with a Michelin-starred meal.

Days Required(Avg): 5-7 Days

THINGS TO DO

    Explore Iconic Landmarks

    1. Burj Khalifa:

  • Visit the world's tallest building and enjoy panoramic views from its observation decks on the 124th, 125th, or 148th floors.

    • 2. Dubai Frame

  • A massive picture frame offering breathtaking views of old and new Dubai.

    • 3. Palm Jumeirah

  • A man-made island featuring luxury resorts, beaches, and the Atlantis Hotel.

    • 4. Visit Shopping and Entertainment Destinations

  • Dubai Mall: One of the largest malls in the world, featuring an aquarium, ice rink, and endless shopping.
  • Mall of the Emirates: Known for Ski Dubai, an indoor ski resort.
  • Souks: Explore the Gold Souk and Spice Souk for traditional shopping experiences

    Cultural Experiences, and Outdoor Activities

    1. Cultural Experiences

  • Al Fahidi Historical District: Experience old Dubai’s charm with museums, art galleries, and traditional architecture.
  • Dubai Creek: Take an abra (traditional boat) ride and visit the Dubai Museum.
  • Jumeirah Mosque: A stunning example of Islamic architecture, open to non-Muslim visitors.

    • 2. Adventure and Outdoor Activities

  • Desert Safari: Experience dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional Bedouin-style dinners.
  • Dubai Marina: Go for a yacht cruise or stroll along the promenade.
  • Skydiving: Tandem jumps over Palm Jumeirah for thrill-seekers.

    • 3. Beaches and Waterparks

  • JBR Beach: Relax by the beach or enjoy watersports.
  • AquaFun Water Park: A giant inflatable water park in the sea.
  • Atlantis Aquaventure: One of the best waterparks in the world.

    Day trips, Fine Dining and Nightlife

    1.Day Trips

  • Abu Dhabi: Visit the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque and Ferrari World.
  • Hatta: Go hiking, kayaking, or explore the Hatta Heritage Village.

    • 2. Fine Dining and Nightlife

  • Burj Al Arab: Indulge in luxury dining at this iconic 7-star hotel.
  • Global Village: Seasonal shopping, dining, and cultural experiences from around the world.
  • Dubai Fountain Show: Enjoy the dazzling evening water and light show at Burj Khalifa Lake.
