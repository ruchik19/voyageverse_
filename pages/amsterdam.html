AMSTERDAM

IFrom its picturesque canals and bridges to its historic homes, Amsterdam could be considered straight out of a fairytale (and the brightly-coloured bicycles and tulip stands around town don’t hurt either). Must-see sights include the Anne Frank House, the Van Gogh Museum, and the world's only floating flower market.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Explore the Canals

  • Take a canal cruise to enjoy the city's iconic waterways. Walk along the canals and admire the historic architecture.

    • 2. Visit the Museums

  • Rijksmuseum: Home to works by Dutch masters like Rembrandt and Vermeer. Van Gogh Museum: Features an extensive collection of Van Gogh’s art. Anne Frank House: Learn about Anne Frank’s life and the impact of World War II

    • 3. Stroll Through the Jordaan Neighborhood

  • Wander the picturesque streets lined with boutique shops, cafes, and art galleries..

    4. Discover Dam Square

  • Visit the Royal Palace and enjoy the lively atmosphere with street performers.

    6. Experience Amsterdam’s Markets

  • Albert Cuyp Market: Great for local food and souvenirs..
  • Bloemenmarkt: A floating flower market showcasing the famous Dutch tulips.

    • 7. Cycle Around the city

  • Rent a bike and explore Amsterdam like a local..

    8. Taste Dutch Delicacies

  • Try stroopwafels, poffertjes, raw herring, and bitterballen.

    • 9. Visit the Red Light District

  • Learn about the area's history or join a guided tour for a deeper understanding..

    • 10. Take a Day Trip

  • Zaanse Schans: Explore traditional windmills and Dutch crafts.
  • Keukenhof Gardens (seasonal): Famous for tulip displays.
  • Volendam and Marken: Charming fishing villages
