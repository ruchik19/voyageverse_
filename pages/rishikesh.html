RISHIKESH

The holy city of Rishikesh, in the base of the Himalayas, holds deep cultural and spiritual significance for local Hindus. Sacred rivers and mountains set the scene for yoga and reflective hikes, and rafting here is an absolute must. The Beatles got in touch with their Eastern spiritual side here, writing several songs during a 1968 stint at a local ashram.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Adventure Activities

  • River Rafting: Enjoy thrilling white-water rafting in the Ganges River.
    Camping: Stay at riverside camps for an immersive nature experience.
    Trekking: Explore nearby trails like the Neer Garh Waterfall Trek or Kunjapuri Temple Trek.
    Bungee Jumping: Experience India’s highest bungee jump at Jumpin Heights.
    Rock Climbing and Rappelling: Popular in the hilly terrains around Rishikesh.

    • 2. Spiritual Activities

  • Ganga Aarti: Witness the mesmerizing evening aarti at Triveni Ghat.
    Visit Ashrams: Parmarth Niketan: Known for yoga and meditation programs.
    Beatles Ashram: Famous for its connection with the Beatles and graffiti art.
    Meditation and Yoga Retreats: Take classes or join a retreat to rejuvenate

    Cultural, Spritual Experiences and Relaxation

    3. Sightseeing

  • Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula: Iconic suspension bridges offering stunning river views.
    Neer Garh Waterfall: A scenic spot perfect for hiking and relaxing.
    Trayambakeshwar Temple: A famous 13-storey temple near Laxman Jhula.
    Kunjapuri Temple: Known for sunrise views and religious significance.

    • 4. Cultural Exploration

  • Attend the International Yoga Festival: Usually held in March.
    Local Markets: Shop for handicrafts, spiritual items, and Ayurvedic products

    5. Wildlife and Nature

  • Rajaji National Park: A great spot for bird watching and wildlife safaris.
    Shivpuri: Known for its lush greenery and camping options.

    • 6.Cafés and Food

  • Explore vegan and organic cafes like Little Buddha Café or Ganga Beach Café for delicious food and serene views.

    • BEST TIME TO VISIT

  • September to November: For pleasant weather and outdoor activities.
    March to May: Ideal for rafting and camping.
    December to February: Cooler temperatures for spiritual retreats and sightseeing.
