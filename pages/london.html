LONDON

London’s a sprawling city at the centre of everything: art, history, culture—you name it. But what sets it apart from other major hubs are its distinct neighbourhoods, each with their own vibe. Spend an afternoon with the fam in Kensington: It’s got museums, parks, and plenty of other kid-approved things to do. Or check out edgy Shoreditch for cool shops and street murals (perfect for photo ops), then head to Soho where you can grab a pint at a pub or hit a club and party ‘til dawn.

Days Required(Avg): 4-7 Days

THINGS TO DO

    Top Attractions

    1. The British Museum

  • Explore a vast collection of artifacts, including the Rosetta Stone and Egyptian mummies (free entry).

    • 2. The Tower of London

  • Discover British history and see the Crown Jewels.

    • 3. Buckingham Palace

  • Witness the Changing of the Guard and admire the royal residence.

    • 4. Big Ben & Houses of Parliament

  • Iconic landmarks along the Thames.

    • 5. The London Eye

  • Enjoy panoramic views of the city from this famous Ferris wheel.

    • 6. Trafalgar Square & National Gallery

  • Explore art and history in the heart of the city.

    • 6. Making of Harry Potter

  • Explore and do a Warner Bros. Studio tour London (Harry Potter Set)

    Cultural Experiences, shopping

    1. Cultural Experiences

  • West End Theatre – Watch world-class musicals and plays.
  • Camden Market – Browse unique shops, vintage goods, and global street food.
  • Witness classical Japanese performances.
  • Covent Garden – Explore markets, street performers, and boutiques.
  • The Shard – Enjoy luxury dining and breathtaking views.
  • Afternoon Tea – Indulge in a quintessential British tradition.

    • 2. Shopping

  • Oxford Street – Over 300 shops, including Selfridges.
  • Harrods – Luxury shopping in Knightsbridge.
  • Portobello Road Market – Famous for antiques and vintage items.

    Day Trips From Tokyo, Parks And Outdoors

    1. Day Trips From London

  • Windsor Castle – The oldest and largest inhabited castle.
  • Stonehenge – A world-famous prehistoric monument.
  • Oxford or Cambridge – Visit iconic university towns.
  • Greenwich – Explore maritime history and the Prime Meridian.

    • 2. Parks and Outdoors

  • Hyde Park – Relax, rent a paddleboat, or visit the Diana Memorial Fountain.
  • Regent’s Park & Primrose Hill – Stroll through beautiful gardens and enjoy scenic city views.
  • Kew Gardens – Explore the world-famous botanical gardens.
