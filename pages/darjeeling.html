THINGS TO DO

    Explore Scenic Spots

    1. Explore Scenic Spots

  • Tiger Hill: Famous for its breathtaking sunrise views over Kanchenjunga and Mount Everest.
  • Batasia Loop: A spiral railway track with panoramic views and a war memorial.
  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train): A UNESCO World Heritage site offering scenic train rides.

    • 2. Visit Iconic Monasteries

  • Ghoom Monastery: Known for its beautiful Buddha statue and spiritual ambiance.
  • Bhutia Busty Monastery: Offers a peaceful retreat and stunning views of the mountains.

    • 3. Tea Gardens

  • Happy Valley Tea Estate: Take a tour to learn about tea processing and sample world-famous Darjeeling tea.

    Adventure , Wildlife and nature

    1. Adventure

  • Trekking: Popular trails include Sandakphu and Singalila Ridge.
  • Rock Climbing: Tenzing Rock and Gombu Rock are great spots for beginners.

    • 2. Wildlife and Nature

  • Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park: Houses rare animals like red pandas and snow leopards.
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (HMI): Features exhibits on mountaineering history and a training center.

    Markets, Excursion, Cultural experiences

    1.Markets

  • Shop for handicrafts, woolen clothing, souvenirs, and authentic Darjeeling tea at Chowk Bazaar and Mall Road.

    • 2. Excursion

  • Mirik: A serene lakeside town, just a short drive from Darjeeling.
  • Lamahatta: A peaceful eco-tourism village offering scenic views and forest trails..

    • 2. Cultural Experiences

  • Attend local festivals like Losar (Tibetan New Year) if you visit at the right time.
  • Enjoy local cuisine at popular cafes and restaurants, such as momos, thukpa, and Nepali thali..
