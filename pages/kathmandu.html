KATHMANDU

Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a vibrant blend of ancient heritage and modern charm. Nestled in a scenic valley surrounded by the Himalayas, it serves as a gateway to Nepalâ€™s rich culture, history, and spirituality. With centuries-old temples, bustling markets, and stunning architecture, Kathmandu offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Visit UNESCO World Heritage Sites:

  • Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple): A famous temple offering panoramic views of the city.
    Pashupatinath Temple: A sacred Hindu temple on the banks of the Bagmati River.
    Boudhanath Stupa: One of the largest stupas in Nepal and an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
    Durbar Square: Historic royal palaces and temples located in the heart of the old city.

    • 2. Explore Thamel:

  • A bustling neighborhood filled with shops, restaurants, and bars, perfect for souvenirs and experiencing Kathmandu's vibrant atmosphere.

    3. Visit Patan and Bhaktapur:

  • Explore these neighboring cities for their ancient temples, unique architecture, and quieter atmosphere compared to Kathmandu. .

    • 4. Hike to Nagarkot:

  • For a day trip or overnight stay, you can enjoy stunning views of the Himalayas, especially at sunrise. .

    5. Take a scenic flight to Mount Everest:

  • If you have the time and budget, a flight that offers spectacular views of Mount Everest and the surrounding peaks is a must-do. .

    • 6. Visit the Garden of Dreams:

  • A peaceful, colonial-era garden perfect for relaxing in the heart of the city.
    Experience Local Culture: Visit local markets like Asan and immerse yourself in the traditional crafts, spices, and local delicacies.
