LEVI

A round-the-year outdoor resort, Levi is one of Finland’s largest ski centers, with 48 slopes mostly for beginners and intermediates, but also including four very difficult black slopes. There’s a full range of other winter activities as well, including snowmobiling, cross country skiing, and dog sledding.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Skiing and Snowboarding

  • Skiing and Snowboarding: Levi is one of Finland's largest ski resorts, with over 40 slopes, making it a popular destination for winter sports enthusiasts.
    Northern Lights Viewing: Levi is a great place to see the Northern Lights, especially during the winter months. Many tours offer aurora viewing experiences.

    • 2. Nothern Lights Viewing

  • Northern Lights Viewing: Levi is a great place to see the Northern Lights, especially during the winter months. Many tours offer aurora viewing experiences.

    • 3. Snowshoeing

  • Snowshoeing and Hiking: Explore the snow-covered landscapes through snowshoeing or winter hiking, or venture out in the summer to enjoy hiking in the natural beauty of the area.

    4. Reindeer and Husky Safaris:

  • Reindeer and Husky Safaris: Experience traditional Lapland culture by going on a reindeer or husky sled ride. These activities offer a unique way to enjoy the Arctic wilderness.

    • 5. Levi Spa and Wellness:

  • Levi Spa and Wellness: After a day on the slopes or exploring, relax in Levi's spa, which offers saunas, hot tubs, and other wellness services. .

    Sightseeing, Cafe's and local Cuisine

    6. Ice Fishing

  • Ice Fishing and Snowmobile Safaris: Try ice fishing on frozen lakes or embark on a snowmobile safari across the wintry terrain. .

    • 7. Levi Village

  • Levi Village: Visit the cozy village center for shopping, dining, and enjoying the local food, including Lappish specialties. .

    • 8. Santa Claus Village

  • Santa Claus Village: Though slightly farther south, the Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi is an excellent day trip from Levi if you're interested in meeting Santa and experiencing the Christmas spirit year-round.
