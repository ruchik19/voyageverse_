GREECE

Greece is the stuff of holiday dreams—with more than 200 islands to find whatever you’re looking for. Want ancient history, art, and culture? Start in the hub of Athens, where you’ll find treasures from the Acropolis to the Parthenon. Brush up on your Greek mythology in Olympia, where you can pay homage to Zeus and walk through the ruins of the first-ever Olympic games

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    Adventure Activities:

    1. Rohtang Pass , Solang Valley

  • Skiing and snowboarding (Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley)..
  • Activities like paragliding, zorbing, and ropeway rides.

    • 2. Beas River

  • Famous for River rafting

    • 3. Hampta Pass and Chandrakhani Pass

  • Trekking trails like Hampta Pass or Chandrakhani Pass.
  • Camping under the stars.

    Cultural, Spritual Experiences and Relaxation

    1. Cultural and Spritual

  • Visit temples (Hadimba, Manu, and Gauri Shankar).
  • Learn about Tibetan culture at monasteries.

    • 2. Relaxation

  • Soak in natural hot springs at Vashisht.
  • Enjoy a quiet picnic by the river.

    Sightseeing, Cafe's and local Cuisine

    1.Sightseeing

  • Visit scenic spots like Kothi Village, Gulaba, and Rahala Waterfalls.
  • Explore old-world charm in villages like Old Manali.

    • 2. Cafe's and local Cuisine

  • Try delicious Himachali dishes like siddu, dham, and trout fish.
  • Chill at cozy cafes in Old Manali.
