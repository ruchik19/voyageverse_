MUMBAI

Mumbai (formerly known as Bombay), India, is famous for its chaotic streets. For bargains and people-watching, outdoor bazaars top the list of attractions. Popular waterfront destinations are Marine Drive, where visitors go to watch the sun set over the Arabian Sea, and the carnival-like Juhu Beach. More sightseeing options are the Gandhi Museum, in the leader's former home, and the cave temples of Elephanta Island. For tranquillity, Mumbai has many religious sites, lakes and parks.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Historical and Cultural exploration

  • Gateway of India: Iconic landmark by the Arabian Sea.
    Elephanta Caves: A UNESCO World Heritage site with rock-cut temples (accessible via ferry from Gateway of India).
    Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CST): Stunning Victorian Gothic architecture.
    Colaba Causeway: Perfect for street shopping and vibrant cafes.

    • 2. Nature and Coastal Charm

  • Marine Drive: A walk or drive along the "Queen's Necklace" at sunset.
    Hanging Gardens: Well-maintained gardens with a view of Marine Drive.
    Girgaon Chowpatty Beach: Enjoy local street food like bhel puri and pav bhaji.
    Sanjay Gandhi National Park: A green escape offering nature trails and the Kanheri Caves.

    3. Art and Bollywood Vibes

  • Prince of Wales Museum (Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya): Fascinating artifacts and art collections.
    Bandra-Worli Sea Link: Modern marvel and breathtaking views.
    Bandra: Street art in Bandra, Mount Mary Basilica, and Carter Road.
    Juhu Beach: Known for its lively atmosphere and Bollywood celebrity sightings.

    • 2. Spiritual and Traditional

  • Haji Ali Dargah: A mosque located on an islet. Siddhivinayak Temple: Famous Ganesh temple. Dhobi Ghat: The world's largest open-air laundromat. .

    5. Shopping and Food Adventures

  • Crawford Market: For fresh produce and quirky finds.
    High Street Phoenix Mall or Palladium: Upscale shopping.
    Local Street Food Tour: Try vada pav, pani puri, and kebabs at Mohammed Ali Road.
    Bollywood Studio Tour: For movie buffs, visit a Bollywood studio to witness the magic of Indian cinema.
