MALDIVES

With 26 atolls and 1,000+ islands spread out across the idyllic waters of the Indian Ocean, the Maldives are an island-hopper’s dream. Below the water, there’s miles of coral reef that are home to thousands of species of marine life and underwater treasures. To get the best of it: Go diving at Broken Rock, snorkel with manta rays in Hanifaru Bay, or charter a boat to hit all the top spots. Then there’s the world above, which is known for its beautiful beaches and luxury villas, but has a rich culture and history to uncover as well..

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Snorkeling and Scuba Diving

  • Explore vibrant coral reefs, underwater caves, and an array of marine life, including manta rays, turtles, and colorful fish. .

    • 2. Private Island Retreats

  • Stay on a private resort island for ultimate luxury and privacy, often with overwater bungalows.

    • 3. Island Hopping

  • Visit local islands, uninhabited islands, or other resort islands for different cultural and scenic experiences. .

    4. Water Sports

  • Engage in activities such as jet skiing, kayaking, paddleboarding, and windsurfing. .

    • 5. Sunset Cruises

  • Enjoy breathtaking sunsets over the Indian Ocean, often accompanied by a dolphin-watching tour.

    7. Spa and Wellness

  • Pamper yourself with spa treatments, yoga sessions, and wellness retreats at luxury resorts.

    • 8. Fishing Trips

  • Try night fishing, big game fishing, or traditional Maldivian fishing methods. .
  • Cultural Experiences – Visit Malé, the capital, for a taste of local history, markets, and architecture.
    With its tranquil setting and diverse activities, the Maldives offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure
