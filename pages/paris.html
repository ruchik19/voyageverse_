PARIS

Paris lives up to its hype: A city with unbelievable food and culture, plus stunning views everywhere you turn. With 18 arrondissements, it’s a lot to see in one trip, but each neighbourhood has a personality all its own. You can’t miss the iconic 7th, where art and history meet—there’s the Eiffel Tower, sure, but the impressive Musée du quai Branly is just a short walk away. It houses an amazing collection of indigenous art. Or, hit up Montmartre (the 18th), with its boho shops and cosy brasseries.

Days Required(Avg): 4-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Iconic Landmarks

  • Louvre Museum: Explore masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: Admire its Gothic architecture and climb the towers for a view.
  • Arc de Triomphe: Visit this monument and enjoy views of the Champs-Élysées.
  • Sacre-Coeur Basilica: Located in Montmartre, it offers stunning city views.

    • 2. Neighborhood Exploration

  • Montmartre: Wander through its bohemian streets, visit artists' studios, and enjoy a café.
  • Le Marais: Known for its historic charm, boutique shopping, and trendy cafes.
  • Latin Quarter: Explore this lively area with its bookstores, bistros, and the Sorbonne.

    3. Cultural Experiences

  • Musée d'Orsay: A must-see for Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art.
  • Palace of Versailles (day trip): Tour the luxurious palace and its gardens.
  • Opera Garnier: Marvel at its stunning interiors.

    • 4. Leisure Activities

  • Seine River Cruise: Enjoy the city’s beauty from the water, especially at sunset.
  • Champs-Élysées: Stroll this iconic avenue and indulge in shopping.
  • Luxembourg Gardens: Relax in these peaceful gardens..

    4. Food & Drink

  • Cafés and Patisseries: Try croissants, macarons, and éclairs.
  • Wine and Cheese Tastings: Experience France’s culinary staples.
  • Parisian Bistros: Enjoy dishes like coq au vin and boeuf bourguignon.

    • 5. Unique Experiences

  • Catacombs of Paris: Explore the underground ossuaries for a spooky adventure.
  • Street Art in Belleville: Discover Paris's vibrant urban art scene.
  • Photography at Trocadéro Gardens: Perfect for capturing the Eiffel Tower
