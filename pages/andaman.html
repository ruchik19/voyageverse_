ANDAMAN AND NICOBAR

Sparsely-populated beaches that give way to an ocean brimming with interesting marine life and coral gardens are just one of the reasons to visit the beautiful Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Ghandi Park, Sippighat Farm and the century-old Cellular Jail (now a pilgrimage destination) provide glimpses into the past and present of these peaceful islands with a mysterious and multicultural past.

Days Required(Avg): 3-5 Days

THINGS TO DO

    1. Beach Activities:

  • Visit stunning beaches like Radhanagar Beach, Laxmanpur Beach, and Corbyns Cove Beach for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

    • 2. Scuba Diving and Snorkeling:

  • Explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life in places like Havelock Island, Neil Island, and North Bay Island.

    • 3. Cellular Jail

  • Visit the historical Cellular Jail in Port Blair, which is now a museum and national memorial dedicated to India's freedom struggle.

    4. Light and Sound Show

  • Attend the evening Light and Sound show at Cellular Jail to learn about its history.

    • 5. Coral Gardens:

  • Visit the glass-bottom boats or take a snorkeling trip to view coral gardens in places like Elephant Beach. .

    • 6. Water Sports

  • Engage in jet-skiing, parasailing, and other water activities, especially at Havelock and Neil Islands.

    7. Visit the Andaman Wildlife Sanctuary

  • Explore the rich flora and fauna in places like the Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park.

    • 8. Explore the Limestone Caves

  • Go to Baratang Island to see the famous limestone caves and mud volcanoes.

    • 9. Tribal Culture:

  • Learn about the indigenous tribes of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, though it's advised to respect their privacy
