TOKYO

With its futuristic skyscrapers, unrivalled food scene, and wild nightlife, Tokyo is a rush of pure adrenaline. The city is famously cutting-edge, yet its ancient Buddhist temples, vintage teahouses, and peaceful gardens offer a serene escape—and a reminder of its past.Spend the morning in Asakusa, Tokyo’s historical neighbourhood, to see the Sensoji temple, then take a stroll along the Sumida River (in the spring, the cherry blossoms are in full force). Or, make your way to Tsukiji Outer Market for a sushi breakfast—the narrow corridors are packed with tons of spots to choose from

Days Required(Avg): 5-7 Days

THINGS TO DO

    Must-See Attractions in Tokyo

    1. Tokyo Tower or Skytree

  • Enjoy panoramic views of the city.
  • Great for photography enthusiasts.

    • 2. Sensō-ji Temple (Asakusa)

  • Tokyo’s oldest and most famous Buddhist temple.
  • Explore the Nakamise shopping street for souvenirs and snacks.

    • 3. Shibuya Crossing

  • Witness one of the busiest pedestrian crossings in the world.
  • Visit the Hachikō Memorial Statue.

    • 4. Harajuku (Takeshita Street)

  • Known for quirky fashion, shops, and colorful treats..
  • Meiji Shrine is nearby for a peaceful retreat.

    • 5. Akihabara

  • A haven for anime, manga, and tech enthusiasts
  • Shop for collectibles and gadgets.

    Cultural Experiences

    1. Sumo Wrestling

  • Attend a match or visit a sumo stable to observe training sessions.

    • 2. Tea Ceremony

  • Experience traditional Japanese hospitality.

    • 3. Kabuki or Noh Theater

  • Witness classical Japanese performances.

    • 4. Try Japanese Cuisine

  • Known for quirky fashion, shops, and colorful treats..
  • Meiji Shrine is nearby for a peaceful retreat.

    Day Trips From Tokyo

    1. Mount Takao (1 hour away)

  • Perfect for hiking and enjoying nature.

    • 2. Nikko (2 hours away)

  • Known for Toshogu Shrine and beautiful landscapes.

    • 3. Kamakura (1 hour away)

  • Famous for the Great Buddha and historic temples.

    • 4. Disneyland or DisneySea

  • A must-visit for families or Disney fans.
